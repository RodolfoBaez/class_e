The UX Design Program is a 6-week program for rising high school juniors and seniors. In this course, I was able to complete the entire cycle of user experience design beginning with understanding users and product issues and ending with a market-ready, clickable design prototype. By the end of the course, I was able to: Conduct user research and define a product strategy Identify target users and issues they encounter with a product Develop solutions to design a product suited for these users Create and test a prototype Package designs into a presentable form for future internship and job opportunities
